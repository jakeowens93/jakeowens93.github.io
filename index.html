About Me

I am a front-end web developer based in Lafayette, Louisiana.

I've been teaching middle school English for the past four years since graduating with my Master of Arts in Teaching from Belmont University.

Now, I'm looking to pivot to web development. The wonderful folks at Treehouse have helped guide me through quite a few projects that have sharpened my skills in HTML, CSS, Sass, JavaScript, and Bootstrap as I completed their Front-End Techdegree.

Reach out to me at jakeowens93@gmail.com or take a look at some of my work below!
